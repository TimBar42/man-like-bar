### III. Visual & Design Assets

#### ‚úíÔ∏è Typography
The brand uses a modern, legible, and slightly blocky typeface that balances the playful humor with the premium streetwear feel.

* **Primary Font:** **Inter** (Used for all display and body text).
    * **Style:** Use a variety of weights (Medium for headings, Regular/Light for body).
* **Logo Treatment:** **Bold, Uppercase, Tracking-Tighter** with the Orange Accent used as a period/dot after the brand name.
    * *Example:* **MAN LIKE BAR.**

#### üì∏ Imagery Style
Imagery should directly connect the product to the pub environment while maintaining a fashion-forward look.

* **Aesthetic:** Cinematic, slightly moody, and real-world (not studio-perfect).
* **Execution:** Products shown being worn in bar/pub settings. High contrast. *The use of a **grayscale filter with a hover-to-color transition*** on the Hero image is a key visual device that must be maintained for all lead photography to emphasize the *premium* yet *raw/authentic* feel.
* **Product Focus:** Focus on fit and fabric texture ("heavyweight cotton").
